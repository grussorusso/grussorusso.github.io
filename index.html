I am a Research Fellow at the Department of Civil Engineering and Computer Science Engineering of the University of Rome Tor Vergata, where I am part of the DAMON research group.

My research interests span the area of distributed computing systems with emphasis on performance optimization and run-time self-adaptation. My current research mainly revolves around:

  • Serverless computing in the Edge-Cloud Continuum
  • Scheduling and resource allocation for data-intensive systems
