I am a Research Associate at the Department of Civil Engineering and Computer Science Engineering of the University of Rome Tor Vergata, where I received my PhD degree in May 2021.

My research interests span the area of distributed computing systems with emphasis on performance optimization and run-time self-adaptation. My current research mainly revolves around:

  • Self-adaptive Data Stream Processing systems
  • QoS-aware Function-as-a-Service at the Edge
  • Workflow Scheduling in the Cloud
